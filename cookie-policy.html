1. Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how LibreSwapNet ("LibreSwapNet", "we", "our", or "us") uses cookies and similar tracking technologies when you access or use our website, applications, and related services.

By continuing to use our platform, you consent to the use of cookies as described in this Policy, unless disabled through your browser or cookie preferences.

2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites function properly, improve user experience, enhance security, and provide analytical information.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the operation of the platform and cannot be disabled. They enable core functions such as authentication, account security, and transaction processing.

Performance and Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how users interact with the platform, allowing us to improve performance and functionality.

Security Cookies

Used to detect suspicious activity, prevent fraud, and maintain account security.

Functional Cookies

These cookies remember user preferences such as language settings, login sessions, and interface customization.

Marketing Cookies

In certain jurisdictions, marketing cookies may be used to personalize content and measure advertising effectiveness.

4. Information Collected Through Cookies

Cookies may collect information including:

  • IP address and approximate location.
  • Browser type and device information.
  • Session identifiers.
  • Pages visited and interaction behavior.
  • Login and authentication activity.
  • Platform performance metrics.

5. Third-Party Cookies

We may use trusted third-party services for analytics, security, fraud prevention, hosting, and performance monitoring.

These third parties may place cookies on your device in accordance with their own privacy policies.

6. Managing Cookies

Users may control or disable cookies through browser settings. However, disabling certain cookies may affect platform functionality and user experience.

  • Chrome: Browser Settings → Privacy and Security
  • Firefox: Preferences → Privacy & Security
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and Site Permissions

7. Legal Basis

Where required by applicable law, we rely on user consent for non-essential cookies. Essential cookies are processed based on legitimate interests and contractual necessity.

8. Data Retention

Cookies may remain on your device temporarily (session cookies) or for a defined period (persistent cookies).

Retention periods depend on the purpose and technical configuration of each cookie.
